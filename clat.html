Get started — free

Thinkplus’ CLAT program aims to assist students in preparing for the CLAT exam and pursuing a career in Law immediately after completing their 12th grade. The program offers a personalized curriculum that enables students to develop a comprehensive understanding of the necessary concepts to excel in the CLAT exam and gain admission to their desired university. Throughout the CLAT prep journey, our team of expert faculty, who possess over 10 years of experience, will provide guidance and support.  To commence the journey, students will undergo an assessment test that evaluates their abilities, strengths, and weaknesses. Following this, there will be a series of concept and application-building classes, as well as extensive mock tests and sectional tests designed to replicate the actual CLAT exam experience. These assessments will enable students to gauge their progress and identify areas for improvement.  Our CLAT course emphasizes the significance of self-evaluation and self-reflection during mock analysis. Furthermore, we offer think-plus enriched extras that aim to assist students in overcoming their weaknesses and enhancing their performance. Additionally, personalized mentoring sessions are available, providing students with the opportunity to discuss their challenges and receive valuable tips, strategies, and insights for the CLAT exam.  Moreover, our CLAT course extends beyond exam preparation. We offer personality and profile-building sessions, a book club, and interactive quizzes to help students reach their maximum potential and achieve their goals. These additional resources aim to develop well-rounded individuals who are not only prepared for the exam but also equipped with the necessary skills for future success in the field of Law.
